Classic Margherita Pizza

A traditional Italian pizza featuring fresh mozzarella, basil, and tomato sauce. This simple yet delicious recipe captures the authentic flavors of Naples and is perfect for pizza lovers of all skill levels.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Cooking time: 12-15 minutes

Number of servings: 4 people

Difficulty level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, combine warm water and yeast. Let stand for 5 minutes until foamy. Add flour, salt, and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough: Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place in a greased bowl, cover with a towel, and let rise for 1 hour.
  3. Shape the pizza: Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C). Punch down the dough and roll it out into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface.
  4. Add toppings: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border. Arrange mozzarella slices on top and drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake: Transfer the pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Finish and serve: Remove from oven, top with fresh basil leaves, and let cool for 2-3 minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips & Notes

Pro Tips:

  • Use a pizza stone for the crispiest crust
  • Let the dough rise in a warm, draft-free area
  • Don't overload the pizza with too many toppings
  • Fresh mozzarella works best for authentic flavor

Note: This recipe can be made vegetarian-friendly and can be customized with additional toppings like mushrooms, bell peppers, or olives.
